Hormonal Disorders

🧬
Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS)
A hormonal disorder causing enlarged ovaries with small cysts.
⚗️
Thyroid Disorders
Imbalances in thyroid hormones affecting menstrual cycles.

Pain-related Diseases

🔴
Endometriosis
Tissue similar to the uterine lining grows outside the uterus.
🌡️
Dysmenorrhea
Severe menstrual cramps and pain during the menstrual cycle.

Bleeding Disorder

🩸
Menorrhagia
Abnormally heavy or prolonged menstrual bleeding.
